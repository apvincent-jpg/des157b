Comparative Analysis

Instagram

Instagram has notifications for almost everything. It lets you know not just when people follow you or send you a message, but when someone you follow has posted or when someone has liked your post. Furthermore, as users leisurely scroll through Instagram, advertisements pop up in their feed, sneaking in in the same format as instagram posts. I want ads to pop up in that same kind of way on the screen.

Nod Coding Site

The Nod Coding site is an academic site that uses very interactive and modern UI/UX, which makes exploring the site super fun. I want to include a lot of highly interactive elements on my page, and make page transitions as seamless as possible to make the page feel very futuristic. Also, interactivity makes users want to explore the site more and stay on it, which is what I am trying to achieve.
