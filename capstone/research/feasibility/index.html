Clearly identify any difficulties you have uncovered with the development of your project that will change the project significantly since the proposal

My project goals have always stayed the same, but I have had to adjust my user flow and methods a couple times to help achieve this goal. Initially, I wanted to create a site that felt like a real online learning experience. I wanted things like ads and notifications to pop up and distract the user, but notifications and ads work best in real life because they are personalized, which is something I cannot do on my app. Although, I can elicit the same feeling of distraction upon clicking on a notification. For example, what if when a user clicks on a notification their screen floods with notifications. Or when they click on an ad, they are taken down a rabbit hole. I realized I don’t have to make the experience completely realistic. I can just think about the feelings I want the user to feel.
